Pizza

Description

A simple and quick homemade pizza can be made using a straightforward dough recipe and your choice of toppings. This process involves a short rise time for the dough and bakes in about 15-20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • For the Dough:
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tbsp instant or active dry yeast
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1/2 cup warm water (around 105-110째F or 40-43째C)
  • For the Toppings:
    • 1/4 cup pizza sauce
    • 1-2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Desired toppings (e.g., pepperoni, sliced bell peppers, onions, mushrooms)

Steps

  1. Make the Dough: In a large bowl, combine the warm water, sugar, and yeast, letting it sit for a few minutes until frothy (if using active dry yeast). Stir in the flour, salt, and olive oil, and mix until a slightly sticky dough forms. Knead gently for a minute or two
  2. Rise: Place the dough in an oiled bowl, turn it over to coat, and cover with a damp towel. Let it rise for 20-25 minutes in a warm spot until it doubles in size.
  3. Assemble: Punch down the dough and roll or stretch it out with your fingertips to fit a greased or cornmeal-dusted baking pan. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the base, leaving a small border for the crust.
  4. Top & Bake: Add your desired cheese and toppings. Preheat your oven to 400째F (200째C). Bake for approximately 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Serve: Slice and serve your homemade pizza hot.
