Protein Shake

Description

This is the protein smoothie recipe you can use to make a delicious and nutritious breakfast shake every single time. Combine Whey Protein with fresh fruit and milk for a tasty start to the day.

Ingredients

  • 250ml milk
  • 1 scoop Whey Protein
  • 1 banana
  • Mixed berries

Steps

  1. Simply blend all the ingredients together, pour into a glass, and enjoy.
