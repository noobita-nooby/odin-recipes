Sandwich

Description

This simple recipe is quick and perfect for any time you want a warm, comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your preferred bread (white or whole grain work well)
  • 1 tablespoon of butter (softened to room temperature for easier spreading)
  • 2-3 slices of cheese (cheddar or American cheese melts nicely)

Steps

  1. Spread the butter evenly on one side of each slice of bread. This is the side that will face outwards during grilling to make it crispy and golden.
  2. Place the cheese slices between the two pieces of bread, with the buttered sides facing outwards.
  3. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, until the bread is golden brown and the cheese has completely melted.
